Demand for different blood types

We maintain a regular supply of all blood types to ensure the right blood is available to everyone who needs it. We need enough of the right types of blood to meet day-to-day patient needs and to cope with emergencies.

02

Which blood types are in demand?

* it's estimated that around 8% of people have O negative blood, but O negative makes up 13% of requests from hospitals.

* Anyone can receive the red cells from O negative donors. So O negative red cells can be used in emergencies or when a person's blood type in unknown.

* We sometimes contact regular donors with the particular blood type we need, and ask them to give blood.
